|[OverHeatState](#user-content-projectscommonOverHeatState)|7|0x7|Overheat state from product|
|[Controller](#user-content-projectscommonController)|8|0x8|Notify the device about the state of the controller application.|
|[WifiSettings](#user-content-projectscommonWifiSettings)|9|0x9|Wifi settings commands|
|[WifiSettingsState](#user-content-projectscommonWifiSettingsState)|10|0xa|Wifi settings state from product|
|[Mavlink](#user-content-projectscommonMavlink)|11|0xb|Mavlink flight plans commands|
|[MavlinkState](#user-content-projectscommonMavlinkState)|12|0xc|Mavlink flight plans states commands|
|[Calibration](#user-content-projectscommonCalibration)|13|0xd|Calibration commands|
|[CalibrationState](#user-content-projectscommonCalibrationState)|14|0xe|Status of the calibration|

|[GPS](#user-content-projectscommonGPS)|16|0x10|GPS related commands|
|[FlightPlanState](#user-content-projectscommonFlightPlanState)|17|0x11|FlightPlan state commands|

|[FlightPlanEvent](#user-content-projectscommonFlightPlanEvent)|19|0x13|FlightPlan Event commands|
|[Audio](#user-content-projectscommonAudio)|20|0x14|Audio_related commands.|

|[Headlights](#user-content-projectscommonHeadlights)|22|0x16|Controls the headlight LEDs of the Evo variants.|
